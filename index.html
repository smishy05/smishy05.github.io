  • Distibuted Systems
  • Internet of Things
  • Security

  • Smart Contracts

  • Prof. Raja Jurdak (Queensland University of Technology): Integration of blockchain and IoT as well as blockchain scalability
  • Prof. Mohammad Hamdaqa (École Polytechnique de Montréal): Chatbot for generating smart contracts from natural language
  • Prof. Mainack Mondal (Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur): Study of privacy hazards in web, especially in Amazon marketplace
  • Prof. Sanand Dilip Amita Athalye (Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur): Distributed image regeneration in Cyber-Physical Systems (Master's Thesis)
